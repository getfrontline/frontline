HashScan Verify Upload Guide
============================
Detected System: Foundry

- Counter
  File: verify-bundles/Counter/metadata.json
  Address: <set env COUNTER_ADDRESS or pass Counter=0xAddress>
  Upload to: https://hashscan.io/<networkType>/contract/<address> -> Verify

- FrontlinePool
  File: verify-bundles/FrontlinePool/metadata.json
  Address: <set env FRONTLINEPOOL_ADDRESS or pass FrontlinePool=0xAddress>
